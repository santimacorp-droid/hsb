DESIGN:      Quantitative Descriptive- Correlational Research Design

RESPONDENTS: 361 students from the University of La Salette, Inc. (Freshmen, Sophomores, and Juniors)

LOCALE:      University of La Salette, Inc. Santiago City, Isabela

TOOLS:       5-Point Likert Scale Survey Questionnaire (Cronbach's alpha = 0.953)

COLLECTION:  Approval → Validation → Pilot Test → Survey → Data Encoding

ANALYSIS:    Frequency & Percentage
             Mean & Standard Deviation
             Mann–Whitney U Test
             Kruskal–Wallis H Test
             Spearman's Rank Correlation
