Drafting (Demographics + 5-Point Likert Scales) ──> Expert Panel Review ──> Pilot Test (Cronbach's Alpha = 0.953) ──> Numeric Coding (1-5 Scale)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5. DATA COLLECTION PROCEDURE (Field Protocol)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Adviser clearance ──> Expert validation ──> Pilot trial ──> Classroom distribution (3–5 min) ──> Immediate retrieval ──> Excel entry

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6. DATA ANALYSIS PROCEDURE (Descriptive & Inferential)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Excel Entry ──> Shapiro-Wilk Normality Test (p < 0.05; Non-Normal) ──> Nonparametric Tests:
* Profile: Frequency & Percentages
* Variable Levels: Mean & Standard Deviation
* Group Differences: Mann-Whitney U (Sex) | Kruskal-Wallis H (Year & Department)
* Relationship: Spearman's rho correlation (Alpha = 0.05 significance)
